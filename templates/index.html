MMU History

From Malaysia’s first private university to a tech hub

Founded in 1996, MMU was Malaysia’s first private university, growing into a hub for ICT, engineering and digital creativity with campuses in Cyberjaya and Melaka.

Over the decades, MMU has supported thousands of STEM graduates, many of whom now lead teams in multinational companies, start-ups, and research labs. The Engineering and Technology community continues this legacy through student-led projects, competitions, and industry-linked capstones.
