About Multimedia University

Malaysia's Leading Private University in STEM & Innovation

Established in 1996, Multimedia University (MMU) is Malaysia's first private university and a pioneer in telecommunications and multimedia education. With campuses in Cyberjaya and Melaka, MMU continues to drive innovation and produce industry-ready graduates.

🏛️

Est. 1996

Founded by Telekom Malaysia, MMU was the first university established in the Multimedia Super Corridor (MSC). Over 25 years of academic excellence in engineering, IT, and creative multimedia.

🎓

11 Faculties

From Engineering & Technology to Computing & Informatics, Creative Multimedia, Business, and Law — MMU offers 100+ programs across undergraduate and postgraduate levels.

🌐

2 Campuses

Cyberjaya campus focuses on engineering, IT, and media arts. Melaka campus specializes in business, law, and creative technology — together serving 20,000+ students.

🏆

Top Rankings

Rated 5-Star by SETARA (Malaysian university rankings), QS-rated, and recognized globally for research excellence in AI, IoT, robotics, and telecommunications.

Main Campus

Cyberjaya

Located in Malaysia's Silicon Valley, the Cyberjaya campus is home to the Faculty of Engineering & Technology, Faculty of Computing & Informatics, and the Faculty of Creative Multimedia.

Melaka
